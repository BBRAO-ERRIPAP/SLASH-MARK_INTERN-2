Projects

Smart Wheel Chair 1

The Smart Wheelchair project is designed to enhance the mobility and independence of individuals with physical disabilities. This innovative wheelchair incorporates a Bluetooth module, allowing users to control the wheelchair using a smartphone or a dedicated Bluetooth remote control. The project leverages modern technology to provide a more convenient and user-friendly experience compared to traditional wheelchairs.

Smart street light with auto intensity control

The Smart Street Light project aims to modernize traditional street lighting systems by integrating smart technologies. This project focuses on enhancing energy efficiency, reducing maintenance costs, and improving public safety through intelligent control and monitoring of street lights. The system uses sensors, microcontrollers, and communication modules to automate and optimize street light operations.
